Home Box — Shipping \& Delivery Policy



(Customer Support \& AI Reference Version)



&nbsp;What delivery options does Home Box offer?



Home Box provides multiple delivery options depending on the product type and delivery location:



Scheduled Delivery (for select furniture)



2-Day Express Delivery (for select furniture)



Same Day Delivery (select locations only)



Standard Delivery (all other products)



Available options and estimated timelines are displayed during checkout.



&nbsp;Can I choose a delivery date and time?



Yes, for select Home Box furniture items:



Customers can select a delivery date and time slot during checkout



Availability depends on the product and location



Additional delivery charges may apply and are shown before payment



&nbsp;What is Same Day Delivery and where is it available?

Same Day Delivery conditions:



Available in select areas of Dubai, Sharjah, and Ajman



Orders must be placed before 12:00 PM on business days



An additional AED 18 delivery charge applies



If these conditions are not met, the order will be processed under standard delivery timelines.



&nbsp;How long does standard delivery take?



Orders placed before 3:30 PM (Saturday–Thursday)

→ Delivered the next business day



Orders placed after 3:30 PM or on Friday

→ Delivered within 2–3 business days



Delivery may take longer for remote or restricted areas



Final delivery timelines are confirmed at checkout.



&nbsp;What happens if I miss my delivery?

Scheduled Delivery



Customer Service will contact the customer to reschedule



Other Delivery Types



The delivery partner will attempt to contact the customer



Up to three delivery attempts will be made



If all attempts fail, the order will be returned to Home Box



Additional charges may apply for rescheduling, depending on the case.



&nbsp;Are there delivery location restrictions?



Yes.



Home Box does not deliver to free zones



Delivery availability is determined by the address entered at checkout



Customers are responsible for ensuring address accuracy.



&nbsp;How much does delivery cost?

Standard Delivery



Free delivery for household orders of AED 500 or more



AED 18 delivery fee for orders below AED 500



Same Day Delivery



Additional AED 18 charge



This fee is refunded only if Home Box fails to deliver on the same day



Cash on Delivery



Additional AED 10 service charge applies



All delivery charges are clearly displayed during checkout.



&nbsp;When is the Same Day Delivery fee refunded?



The Same Day Delivery fee (AED 18) is refunded only if:



Home Box fails to deliver on the same day due to internal reasons



The fee is not refundable if:



Delivery fails because the customer is unavailable



The delivery address is incorrect



&nbsp;Escalation \& Manual Review Rules (Critical)

The case must be escalated to human support if:



Delivery is delayed beyond the promised timeline



Customer claims non-delivery but system shows delivered



Delivery partner error is reported



Customer requests compensation or exceptions



Address disputes or rescheduling charges are contested



Same Day Delivery refund eligibility is disputed



AI Response Rule:



“This delivery case requires manual review by our support team. I’ll escalate it for you.”



&nbsp;Important Policy Notes



Delivery timelines may vary due to location, stock availability, or operational constraints



Home Box reserves the right to modify delivery options, charges, or timelines



Final delivery details shown at checkout take precedence
