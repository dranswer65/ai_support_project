Home Box — Return & Refund Policy

(Customer Support & AI Reference Version)

 What is the return eligibility period?

Products may be returned within 30 days from the date of delivery or purchase.

To be eligible:

The original invoice must be provided

Items must be unused

Items must be undamaged

Items must be in original packaging

 Which items are NOT eligible for return?

The following items cannot be returned or exchanged:

Mattresses

Bedding accessories

Clearance or sale items

Customized or made-to-order products

Items damaged due to misuse, negligence, or improper handling

 What if the product has a manufacturing defect?

Products with manufacturing defects may be returned.

Process:

The item is inspected by the Home Box team

If the defect is confirmed:

A refund or replacement will be processed

Approval is subject to inspection results.

 How can I return a product?
✔ In-Store Returns

Products purchased online or in-store can be returned at any Home Box store

The original invoice must be presented

✔ Home Pickup Returns

Home pickup can be arranged for eligible items

Pickup is usually scheduled within 3–5 business days

Availability depends on location and item type

 How will I receive my refund?
✔ Card Payments

Refunded to the original card

Processing time: 7–14 business days (bank dependent)

✔ Cash Purchases

Refunded in-store

Amounts exceeding store limits may be refunded via Shukran Pay

✔ Shukran Pay / Wallet

Refund credited to the customer’s Shukran account

Credits can be used for future purchases

 How is the refund amount calculated?

Refunds are adjusted based on services already completed.

Example:

Product value: AED 500

Delivery fee: AED 60

Assembly fee: AED 20

If delivery and assembly were completed:
➡ Refundable amount = AED 420

 Delivery, assembly, and service fees are non-refundable once completed.

 Can I exchange a product instead of returning it?

Yes, exchanges are allowed if:

The item is eligible for return

The request is within 30 days

Stock is available

Price differences must be paid by the customer if applicable.

 Do online orders follow different rules?

No. Online orders follow the same return eligibility rules.

Refunds are issued using the original payment method

Guest users may receive refunds via card or Shukran Pay, depending on payment type

 Are delivery charges refundable?

Delivery charges are non-refundable once the service is completed

Free shipping promotions may not apply to returned or exchanged items

 Escalation & Manual Review Rules (Critical)
The case must be escalated to human support if:

Refund eligibility is disputed by the customer

Product condition cannot be clearly verified

The return request falls outside the 30-day window

The customer requests an exception to policy

Multiple policies apply and outcome is unclear

The customer disagrees with inspection results

AI Response Rule:

“This case requires manual review by our support team. I’ll escalate it for you.”

 Final Authority & Policy Notes

Home Box reserves the right to refuse returns that do not meet policy conditions

All returns and refunds are subject to inspection and approval

Policies may vary by country or store location

Final decisions rest with Home Box management
