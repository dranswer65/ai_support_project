Home Box — Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)



(Customer Support \& AI Reference Version)



&nbsp;When will I receive my order?



Delivery timelines depend on the product type and delivery option selected at checkout.



Select Home Box Furniture



2-Day Express Delivery (additional charges apply)



Scheduled Delivery (choose a preferred date and time; charges vary by location)



All Other Products



Orders placed before 12:00 PM (business days)

→ Same-day delivery to select locations in Dubai, Sharjah, and Ajman (additional AED 18)



Orders placed before 3:30 PM (Saturday–Thursday)

→ Delivered the next business day



Orders placed after 3:30 PM or on Friday

→ Delivered within 2–3 business days



Delivery timelines may change due to weather, stock availability, or logistics. Customers will be contacted if rescheduling is required.



&nbsp;How do I select a delivery date and time for furniture?



For eligible Home Box furniture:



Add the product to your cart



Pin your delivery location (optional but recommended)



Select an available delivery date from the calendar



Choose a suitable delivery time slot



The selected delivery date and time will be confirmed at checkout.



&nbsp;Can I change my delivery address after placing an order?



Delivery addresses cannot be changed online after placing an order.



However:



You may request an address change when contacted by the delivery partner



Address changes may result in delivery delays or additional charges



&nbsp;What payment methods are accepted?



Home Box accepts the following payment methods:



Credit \& Debit Cards (UAE-issued and international Visa/Mastercard)



Cash on Delivery



My Credit



Gift Cards



Shukrans (minimum 200 points required)



Tabby



Accepted payment options are displayed during checkout.



&nbsp;How do I cancel my order?



If you are logged into your account:



Go to My Account → Order Details



Select the relevant order



Choose the items you wish to cancel



Click Cancel selected items



Cancellation eligibility depends on order status.



&nbsp;Can I place an order without creating an account?



Yes. Customers may shop as guest users.



Only delivery and payment details are required to complete checkout.



&nbsp;Can I order an out-of-stock product?



No. Out-of-stock items cannot be ordered.



You may use the “Notify Me” option on the product page to receive an alert when the item becomes available again.



&nbsp;How can I return an order?



Returns can be initiated through:



Online return request (via My Account)



Return at a Home Box store (original invoice required)



Pickup request via customer support



Returned items must be:



In original condition



In original packaging



Detailed rules are covered in the Return \& Refund Policy document.



&nbsp;Will my return be accepted without original packaging?



No. Returns are accepted only if the product is returned in its original packaging.



&nbsp;How long do refunds take?



Refund timelines vary by payment method:



Product receipt \& inspection: 2–3 business days



Quality check \& refund initiation: ~2 business days



Bank processing: 2–15 business days (bank dependent)



&nbsp;What is Click \& Collect?



Click \& Collect allows customers to:



Shop and pay online



Select a participating Home Box store



Collect the order at their convenience



This service is complimentary.



&nbsp;How do I join the Shukran loyalty program?



To join Shukran:



Sign up on the Home Box website



Go to My Account → Shukran



Follow the on-screen instructions



&nbsp;How do I earn Shukrans online?



Create or log into your Home Box account



Link your Shukran membership under My Account



Earn points automatically on eligible purchases
